Engineering Entrance Exams

Get into top engineering colleges like IITs, NITs, BITS, and state colleges

JEE Main

After 12th

Joint Entrance Examination Main

Admission to NITs, IIITs, GFTIs and other engineering colleges

Conducting Body: National Testing Agency (NTA)
Website: jeemain.nta.nic.in
Eligibility: 12th with PCM, 75% marks
Age Limit: No age limit
Attempts: 3 consecutive years

Exam Pattern

Mode Computer Based Test
Duration 3 hours
Subjects Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
Total Marks 300
Negative Marking Yes

Important Dates (Tentative)

Registration Dec - Jan
Exam Feb - Apr
Result April

Preparation Tips

  • Focus on NCERT books for strong fundamentals
  • Practice previous year question papers
  • Take regular mock tests for time management
  • Revise formulas and concepts regularly

Career Paths

BTech from NITs BE from GFTIs BArch BPlanning

JEE Advanced

After 12th

Joint Entrance Examination Advanced

Admission to Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs)

Conducting Body: IITs (Rotating Basis)
Website: jeeadv.ac.in
Eligibility: Top 2,50,000 in JEE Main
Age Limit: Max 25 years
Attempts: 2 attempts in consecutive years

Exam Pattern

Mode Computer Based Test
Duration 3 hours each paper (2 papers)
Subjects Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
Total Marks 360
Negative Marking Yes

Important Dates (Tentative)

Registration May
Exam June
Result June

Preparation Tips

  • Strong conceptual understanding required
  • Focus on problem-solving speed
  • Practice advanced mathematics
  • Solve IIT JEE specific coaching material

Top IITs

IIT Bombay IIT Delhi IIT Madras IIT Kanpur IIT Kharagpur

Medical Entrance Exams

Pursue careers in Medicine, Dentistry, and Healthcare fields

NEET UG

After 12th

National Eligibility cum Entrance Test Undergraduate

Admission to MBBS, BDS, AYUSH courses across India

Conducting Body: National Testing Agency (NTA)
Website: neet.nta.nic.in
Eligibility: 12th with PCB, 50% marks
Age Limit: 17-25 years
Attempts: No limit till age limit

Exam Pattern

Mode Pen and Paper
Duration 3 hours 20 minutes
Subjects Physics, Chemistry, Biology
Total Marks 720
Negative Marking Yes

Important Dates (Tentative)

Registration Mar - Apr
Exam May
Result June

Preparation Tips

  • NCERT books are essential for preparation
  • Practice diagrams and labeling for biology
  • Regular revision of all three subjects
  • Take mock tests to improve speed

Defense Services Exams

Serve the nation through Army, Navy, and Air Force

NDA

After 12th

National Defence Academy

Entry to Army, Navy, Air Force wings of NDA

Conducting Body: UPSC
Website: upsc.gov.in
Eligibility: 12th Pass (Science for Air Force/Navy)
Age Limit: 16.5 - 19.5 years
Attempts: 6 attempts

Selection Process

1 Written Examination
2 SSB Interview (5 days)
3 Medical Examination
4 Final Merit List

Training

3 years at NDA Khadakwasla + 1 year at respective service academy

Career Options

Army Officer Navy Officer Air Force Officer Higher Command
