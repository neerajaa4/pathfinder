Science Stream

For students interested in Technology, Medicine, Research and Innovation

📚 Core Subjects

Physics Study of matter, energy, and their interactions
Chemistry Study of substances and their transformations
Mathematics Advanced mathematical concepts and applications
Biology Study of living organisms and life processes

🔬 Popular Combinations

PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics)

  • Engineering
  • Architecture
  • Computer Science
  • Defense Services

PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)

  • MBBS - Medicine
  • BDS - Dentistry
  • Pharmacy
  • Biotechnology

PCMB (All Four Subjects)

  • Widest career options
  • Both Engineering & Medical
  • Research Fields
  • Flexible career choices

Medical

  • BDS, BAMS, BHMS

Other Degrees

  • BPharm (4 years)

After Degree →

Post Graduation
  • MTech/MS (Engineering)
  • MD/MS (Medical)

  • MBA (Management)

Final Careers →

Engineering Fields

Software Engineer, Mechanical Engineer, Civil Engineer, Data Scientist

Medical Fields

Doctor, Surgeon, Dentist, Pharmacist, Medical Researcher

Research & Development

Scientist, Research Analyst, Lab Technician, Professor

Commerce Stream

For students interested in Business, Finance, Accounting and Entrepreneurship

📚 Core Subjects

Accountancy Principles of accounting and financial management
Business Studies Study of business operations and management
Economics Micro and macro economic principles
Mathematics/Statistics Optional based on course selection

💼 Career Paths After Commerce

Professional Courses →

CA (Chartered Accountancy)
  • Duration: 4.5-5 years
  • Exams: CA Foundation, Intermediate, Final
  • Scope: Auditing, Taxation, Finance
CS (Company Secretary)
  • Duration: 3-4 years
  • Corporate legal compliance
  • High demand in corporates
CMA (Cost Management Accountant)
  • Duration: 3-4 years
  • Cost accounting specialization
  • Manufacturing sector focus

Degree Courses →

BCom (Bachelor of Commerce)
  • Duration: 3 years
  • Specializations: Accounting, Finance
  • Further studies: MBA, MCom
BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
  • Duration: 3 years
  • Management focused
  • Path to MBA
Economics Honours
  • Duration: 3 years
  • Research and analysis
  • Banking and policy roles

🏢 Job Opportunities

Accounting & Finance

Accountant, Financial Analyst, Auditor, Tax Consultant

Salary: ₹3-12 LPA
Banking & Insurance

Bank PO, Relationship Manager, Insurance Advisor

Salary: ₹4-15 LPA
Corporate Sector

HR Manager, Marketing Executive, Business Analyst

Salary: ₹4-20 LPA
